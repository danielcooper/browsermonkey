1      Introduction

 

We received feedback when giving in our (300 page) acceptance criteria document that our tests were more extensive than they needed to be. For this reason, combined with time and printing constraints, we have not documented every test with a screenshot. Instead, for each testing section we have a table showing the individual tests, and only for whole testing sections do we show a selection of screenshots. Most sections need only one screenshot example as the tests are very similar within each section.

 

The programmers have annotated any fails with explanations.

 

The numbering of the document matches the numbering of the acceptance criteria, which is why we go to heading 3 after this introduction.
